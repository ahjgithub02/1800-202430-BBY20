Welcome to RemindMe

- Your Ultimate Collaborative Reminder App -

RemindMe is your go-to app for managing tasks and deadlines while staying connected with peers. The Collaborative Live Active Calendar (CLAC) lets group members share assignments and reminders in real time, keeping everyone in sync. Whether in a class or work group, RemindMe keeps you updated on shared tasks while allowing you to organize your personal schedule.
